<h2>Hey! I’m Michael.</h2> <br>

<h3>So, why frontend web development?</h3>
<p>Honestly, I just love watching creative ideas turn into something real that people can actually use. There’s something satisfying about taking a design and making it interactive-where users don’t just see it, they feel it.</p> <br>

<h3>I’m all about:</h3>

<p>- Crafting smooth user experiences. A great website should feel as good to use as it looks. I want every interaction to feel effortless.</p>
<p>- Bringing designs to life. I take visual concepts and turn them into responsive, pixel-perfect interfaces that actually work on any device.</p>
<p>- Building interactive sites. I like making web apps that react to what people do—so using them feels dynamic and memorable.</p>

<p>The web never stands still, and neither do I.</p> 


<h3>Where I Am in My Journey?</h3>

<p>I’m constantly learning and improving. My goal is to keep pushing what’s possible and always stay on top of the latest trends. I love working on fresh, challenging projects, exploring new frameworks, and digging deeper into the tech that powers the web.
